FAQ

Common questions.

Can't find your answer? Email deviai.ofc@gmail.com — we respond within 24 hours.

What is the turnaround time?
+
Standard is 48 hours. Growth clients get 24-hour priority, Elite clients same-day. Every video goes through a full quality review before delivery.
Do I need to be on camera?
+
It depends on your goals. On-camera content performs best for brand-building, but we build full systems using location footage, voiceover, text overlays and lifestyle visuals without you on screen.
What platforms do you optimise for?
+
Instagram (Reels + Feed), TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook and LinkedIn. Each platform receives native formatting, aspect ratios and hooks — nothing repurposed without proper optimisation.
Do you work outside the USA?
+
Yes — USA, UK, Ireland, Dubai/UAE and Spain. Our strategies are adapted to each market's audience behaviour and platform trends.
Is there a contract or lock-in?
+
Starter has no contract — cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Growth requires a 3-month minimum and Elite is 6 months. These minimums protect your investment, as results compound over time.
How do I get started?
+
Book a free 30-minute strategy call below. We'll discuss your market and goals, and onboarding takes 3–5 days — first content drops within the first week.
